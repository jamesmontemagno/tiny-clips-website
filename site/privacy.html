Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Tiny Clips for Mac is developed and maintained by Refractored LLC. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information when you use Tiny Clips.

No Data Collection

Tiny Clips does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or personally identifiable information (PII) of any kind. The app operates entirely on your device and does not send any data to external servers.

What We Don't Collect

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent to third-party services
  • No screen content or captured media
  • No device identifiers

Local Storage

Any captures (screenshots, videos, GIFs) you create with Tiny Clips are saved locally to a folder of your choosing on your Mac. Refractored LLC has no access to these files.

Third-Party Services

Tiny Clips uses Sparkle for automatic software updates. Sparkle may contact our update server to check for new versions; this request includes your app version and macOS version but does not include any personally identifiable information. No user data is transmitted or stored.

Children's Privacy

Tiny Clips does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via GitHub Issues.
